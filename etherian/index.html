Description

Etherian is an adventure-platforming game with puzzle solving elements where you will have to jump, swing, swim, slide, and climb your way through 4 unique biomes. Take your time and find secret items as you explore your environment, or master your skills and speedrun your way through!

History

The first full-release project of a couple that met while making games in university and never stopped, Etherian was started in winter 2016 with the goal of creating a game that really used the strengths of the platform to create an engaging gameplay experience specifically for VR.

Features

  • Wide range of unique mechanics and movement systems
  • Explorable environments with secrets and shortcuts to be found
  • Play at your own pace - master the course, or enjoy the scenery
  • Hours of content: 20 full levels, unlockables, and more to be announced in full release
