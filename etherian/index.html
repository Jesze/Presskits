Description

Etherian is an adventure-platforming game with puzzle solving elements where you will have to jump, swing, swim, slide, and climb your way through the beautiful floating world of Etheria. Traverse the fractured scapes to test your skills, uncover the stories of the ancients, or explore the mystical world around you in search of secrets.

History

The first full-release project of a the two person studio Simulacrum Interactive, Etherian came from the desire to explore a beautiful world in a way that really takes advantage of the ability to move and interact in VR.

Features

  • Get moving: Enjoy a more hands-on way of moving through the world
  • Go where you want: Explorable environments with secrets and shortcuts to be found
  • Play at your own pace: Master the courses, enjoy the scenery, or go off the beaten path
  • Hours of content: 20 levels across 4 biomes, alternate routes, unlockables, easter eggs
